About Aividend

Aividend is a fintech company that offers a comprehensive portfolio management platform for investors looking to build and manage their investment portfolios. Our platform provides users with an intuitive and user-friendly interface that allows them to easily track and analyze their investments, including stocks, ETFs, and mutual funds.

Features

Market News and Analysis
Investment Ideas and Recommendations
Educational Resources

Our Mission

At Aividend, we believe in making investing accessible and easy for everyone, whether they are seasoned investors or new to the world of investing. With our innovative platform, investors can confidently manage their investments and achieve their financial goals.

Target Market

Our target market is individuals who are looking for an affordable and user-friendly way to invest in the market. We also aim to serve customers who are dissatisfied with the high fees and lack of transparency of traditional investment management services.

Subscription-Based Model

We plan to generate revenue through a subscription-based business model, with different tiers of service based on the level of investment and support required. We also plan to expand into B2B services in the future, offering our platform to employers as a workplace benefit.
